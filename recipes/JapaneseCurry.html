Japanese Curry

Description

Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Rice
  • Japanese Curry Block
Steps
  1. First make sure to wash all your vegetables and defrost your chicken
  2. Cut all your veggies into squares and boil them till soft while you begin cooking rice
  3. Once veggies are soft enough you then add the Japanese Curry Block and mix into fully brown
  4. Cook your chicken and add to the veggies
  5. Enjoy with the rice!
