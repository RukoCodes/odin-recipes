Avacado Toast

Description

Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Avacado
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Egg
Steps
  1. Toast you bread
  2. While toasting cook your eggs and add seasoning
  3. Cut your Avacado into slices or smash if you'd like
  4. Add a little seasoming to the avacado
  5. PLace avacado and egg on top of the toast and enjoy!
